# Glossary — Sure Good Foods — Complete Business Profile for Website Design

Quick definitions of key terms and concepts referenced in this investigation.

---

## Pure-Play Commodity Trader

A firm that buys and sells physical food commodities across international borders without owning production, processing, or farming infrastructure. SGF earns margin on sourcing expertise, logistics coordination, documentation handling, and market access rather than on manufacturing. This is the fundamental distinction that shapes every aspect of the website: the value proposition is trust, network, and execution — not factory capacity.

## IQF (Individually Quick Frozen)

A freezing method where individual pieces of food (fruit, vegetables, potato products) are frozen separately rather than in a block, preserving texture, color, and nutritional value. SGF trades a complete line of IQF fruits and vegetables for customers using them as ingredients in further-processed products or in food service applications.

## HGP-Free (Hormone Growth Promotant-Free)

A product certification indicating beef was raised without synthetic growth hormones. A key requirement for EU market access and premium retail buyers. Central to the Argyle Foods Group partnership, alongside grassfed, GMO-free, antibiotic-free, and never-confined designations.

## Bills of Lading (BOL)

Legal documents issued by carriers to shippers detailing the type, quantity, and destination of goods being transported. SGF has 3,585 BOLs on file in US trade databases from August 2017 to March 2026 — a verifiable measure of trade volume and operational activity.

## Anuga

The world's largest food and beverage trade fair, held biennially in Cologne, Germany. In 2025 it hosted 8,000+ exhibitors from 110 countries. SGF exhibited in Hall 06.1, Booth E050 under the Canada Pork banner, positioning itself alongside major Canadian meat exporters.

## Canada Pork International (CPI)

The export trade promotion agency for the Canadian pork industry, representing producers and processors in international markets. SGF's CPI membership and co-exhibition at Anuga under the Canada Pork banner signals deep integration with the Canadian pork export ecosystem.

## Private Label Trading

Facilitating the sale of food products under a retailer's or wholesaler's own brand rather than the producer's brand. SGF's Anuga profile lists private label products for wholesale and retail trade as a distribution channel — a capability not surfaced on their current website.

## GFSI (Global Food Safety Initiative)

An industry body that benchmarks food safety management standards (SQF, BRCGS, FSSC 22000, IFS). Many major retailers and foodservice distributors require GFSI certification from suppliers. SGF does not surface any GFSI or food safety certifications online, which may reflect reliance on supply chain partners' certifications rather than direct facility certification.
